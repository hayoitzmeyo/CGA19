Our Vision

Each year, over 200 million individuals suffer from the detrimental impacts of natural disasters. This simultaneously equates to more than 2 trillion dollars in property damage and economic losses. Homeowners face rising threats from burglary and other hazards, usually with little warning or reliable information. Far too often, families and property owners are left in the dark—unaware of the risks that threaten their safety, security, and financial well-being.

We created Georisk because we believe everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, and actionable risk insights—no matter where they live or what they own. By harnessing the power of advanced technology and decades of data, we’re here to empower you with the knowledge you need to protect what matters most.

We utilize cutting-edge AI technology and nuanced algorithms, including neural networks and predictive models, to deliver accurate and dependable risk evaluations. Our commitment is to provide the best insights possible, so that's why we trained our models on decades of data, using high-level evaluation methods such as multi-layered analysis, real time data-integration, predictive analytics, and much more.

FAQ

What does Georisk provide?

Georisk offers risk assessments using just your home address, providing in-depth, accurate insights to your home's risk factors and possible action items.

How often update data?

Our data is updated monthly.

Is Georisk free to use?

Yes, it is free to use.

What risks do you cover?

We cover wildfire risk, home burglary risk, flooding risk, earthquake risk, and landslide risk.

How accurate is Georisk?

Accuracy is our top priority. Referencing historical and present data, we have successfully mapped the risks of millions of homes.

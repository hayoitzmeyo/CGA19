Meet the Team

Meet the team that put this website together! Pioneered by Bay Area high schoolers Johnson Che, Harnoor Sethi, & Joseph Lim from Bellarmine College Preparatory, this website was created as a way to provide free and accessible geotechnical assessments for everyone!
Johnson Che

Hi, I'm Johnson Che, a rising junior at Bellarmine College Preparatory. I am one of the lead developers for Georisk, constructing the frontend, software framework, prediction algorithms and more. While it has been an interesting experience getting to work on this website, I am motivated to refine this website because I truly hope that it can serve as a way to help others in my community. 

Harnoor Sethi

Hi, my name is Harnoor Sethi, also a rising Junior at Bellarmine College Preparatory, with interdisciplinary interests in AI, coding, and mathematics. As I lead developer for Georisk, I have contributed greatly to creating the neural networks our website uses. To me, this website isn't just another project, it's a way to turn my coding abilities into a helpful service for my community.

Joseph Lim

Greetings, my name is Joseph Lim, and I am proud to have been able to work on Georisk as a lead developer. I have contributed to creating the algorithms for flood and landslide detection, and I hope that this website can help many others!

Features

Wildfire Risk Analysis

We use a custom-built algorithm combining decades of data with information like fuel type, wildfire urban interface proximity, burn risk, and much more to convert your home's risk factors into a raster score. This analysis is highly accurate; built on artificial intelligence trained on fire data, we use quantile normalization to ensure an accurate inspection outcome.

Flooding Risk

We use a bulletproof algorithm to determine your home's risk of flooding. This includes incorporating year-round weather patterns (accounted for global warming), FEMA zone data, elevation, proximity to water bodies, and much more to paint a hyper-accurate picture of your home's risks.

Landslide Risk Inspection

Closely related to earthquakes, landslides are arguably even more dangerous at times. By using algorithms to convert predicted rain intensities, seismic hazard data, bedrock structure, earthquake activity and much more into frequency charts, we provide accurate analyses regarding your home's susceptibilities.

Earthquake Risk Evaluation

While devastating earthquakes don't happen as often in many areas, when they do, their impacts are far-reaching and brutal, causing trillions of dollars in damages. We use AI-powered algorithms incorporating real-world historical density mapping, soil classifications, PGA data and much more to evaluate your home's earthquake risk.

About Us

Mission Statement

Our goal is to empower every homeowner and community with clear, accurate, and actionable risk insights—using the power of advanced technology and data—so that everyone can protect what matters most and confidently face the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Story

In the summer of 2023, a devastating wildfire swept through a quiet neighborhood on the outskirts of the San Francisco Bay Area. Among the thousands of families forced to evacuate, all 3 of us were forced to follow along our parents and watch, waiting, unable to act. When we looked towards the mountains once full of life, we found the fire consuming the forests that we had once visited before. As some of our community members' houses experienced detrimental damages, many struggled to recuperate.

As the community rallied to recollect itself, one question echoed through every conversation:
“Could we really have done something different?”

That question became the spark for Georisk.

We realized that hundreds of millions of people, just like those in our community, make the biggest investments of their lives with little more than hope and a handful of generic reports. But it's not just about fires. It's about earthquakes, floods, landslides, burglary, issues that might seem "random" at first can become predictable and solvable with the help of data science and technology. The data exists, but it’s scattered, complex, and often locked behind paywalls or technical jargon. Families, renters, and even real estate professionals are left in the dark, unable to make truly informed decisions about their safety and future.

Determined to change this, our team came together with a bold vision:
To build a platform that transforms raw data into clear, actionable insights—accessible to everyone, for free.

We spent months gathering decades of historical data, honing our skills, and developing advanced AI models capable of predicting not just wildfires, but also floods, earthquakes, burglaries, and more. Every line of code, every data point, was driven by a simple belief:

Knowledge is power, and everyone deserves it.

If you know your risks, you can own your safety.

Today, Georisk empowers households to make safer, smarter choices, before disaster strikes. Our story is not as tragic as those who suffered devastating losses at the hands of seemingly uncontrollable events, but they laid the foundation of a movement to turn uncertainty into empowerment, and to ensure that no one is ever left in the dark again.
